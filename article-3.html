How to Get Your Harare Business Found on Google

By Invitt Co  ·  March 2026  ·  7 min read
← Back to Blog

Getting your business to appear on Google doesn't require a massive budget or deep technical knowledge. What it requires is understanding how Google works and taking a few consistent steps to show up in front of people who are already looking for what you offer.

This guide is written specifically for businesses in Harare and Zimbabwe. The principles are the same as anywhere in the world, but the opportunities here are even greater because so few local businesses are doing this properly.

The opportunity: Most businesses in Harare are not optimised for Google. That means if you do the basics correctly, you can outrank competitors who have been operating for years — simply because you showed up online and they didn't.

Step by Step: Getting Found on Google

01

Build a Proper Website

Google indexes websites — not Facebook pages or WhatsApp numbers. You need a real website with your own domain name (e.g. yourbusiness.co.zw). It should be mobile-friendly, load quickly, and clearly describe what you do and where you're located. This is the foundation everything else is built on.

02

Claim Your Google Business Profile

This is free and one of the highest-impact things you can do. A Google Business Profile gets your business onto Google Maps and into local search results. Fill it in completely — add photos, your physical address, phone number, business hours, and a description of your services. Ask satisfied customers to leave reviews.

03

Use the Right Keywords

Keywords are the words and phrases people type into Google when looking for businesses like yours. Think about how your customers search — "printer repair Harare", "web designer Zimbabwe", "toner cartridges Harare". Include these phrases naturally in your website's page titles, headings, and body text. Don't stuff them in unnaturally — write for people first, then optimise for Google.

04

Add SEO Meta Tags to Your Website

Meta tags are short pieces of code in your website that tell Google what each page is about. The most important ones are the title tag and meta description — these are what appear in Google search results. Every page of your site should have a unique, descriptive title and a compelling description that includes your target keywords and location.

05

Submit Your Site to Google Search Console

Google Search Console is a free tool that tells Google your website exists and shows you how it's performing in search results. After setting it up, submit a sitemap — a file that lists all your pages so Google can find and index them quickly. This can dramatically speed up how fast your site appears in search results.

06

Get Other Websites to Link to You

When other reputable websites link to yours, Google sees your site as more trustworthy and ranks it higher. Start by getting listed on Zimbabwe business directories, industry associations, and local news sites. Even a few quality links from relevant sites can make a significant difference in your rankings.

07

Create Helpful Content Regularly

Google rewards websites that consistently publish useful, relevant content. A simple blog with articles answering common questions your customers ask is one of the most effective long-term SEO strategies. Each article is another opportunity to appear in search results for different keywords — expanding your reach over time.

How Long Does It Take?

SEO is not instant — it typically takes 3 to 6 months to see significant results. But the results are long-lasting and compounding. Unlike paid advertising which stops the moment you stop paying, good SEO keeps working for you month after month.

The businesses that start SEO today will be the ones dominating Google search results in Harare six months from now. The ones that wait will be playing catch-up.
