Privacy Policy

Invitt Co, Harare, Zimbabwe Effective: January 2026 Last updated: June 2026
Overview

What this policy covers

This Privacy Policy explains what information Invitt Co collects when you visit invitt.co.zw or interact with our AI receptionist Tess, how we use that information, and how we protect it.

We keep it simple: we collect only what we need to help you, we don't sell your data, and we only share it with the tools that power our service.

Data Collection

What we collect

When you chat with Tess or submit an enquiry, we may collect the following:

Data Why we collect it
Full name To address you personally when Lennon follows up
Email address To send project quotes or follow-up communication
Phone / WhatsApp number So Lennon can reach you directly to discuss your project
Business name & type To understand your needs and recommend the right package
Budget range To match you with the most appropriate service tier
Chat messages To provide context for follow-up and improve Tess's responses
We don't collect what we don't need

We do not collect payment card details, government IDs, or sensitive personal data. We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking purposes.

How we use it

What we do with your data

  • Contact you to follow up on your project enquiry
  • Prepare a relevant quote or proposal for your business
  • Book and confirm discovery calls with Lennon
  • Improve Tess's ability to answer common questions accurately
  • Maintain a record of our client conversations for project context

We do not use your data for advertising, remarketing, or any purpose unrelated to delivering our services to you.

Third Parties

Who we share data with

We only share your data with the tools that directly power our service. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information.

Supabase

Our database provider. Your messages, contact details, and booking information are stored securely on Supabase's infrastructure. Supabase Privacy Policy →

OpenRouter / NVIDIA Nemotron

The AI model that powers Tess's responses. Your messages are sent to OpenRouter to generate a response. They are not stored or used to train models beyond what their policy permits. OpenRouter Privacy Policy →

Render

Our backend hosting provider. Tess's server runs on Render's infrastructure. Render Privacy Policy →

Your Rights

Your rights over your data

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your data at any time
  • Opt out of any future communication from us

To exercise any of these rights, contact Lennon directly via email or WhatsApp — details below.

Data Security

How we protect your data

All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in a secured, access-controlled database. Only Lennon has access to your contact details and chat history. We do not expose your information to any unauthorised parties.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no system is completely immune to risk. If you have concerns about a specific piece of information, reach out to us directly.

Updates

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our tools or practices change. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of our site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
