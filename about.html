Meet Lennon.

Your trusted web design and digital growth specialist — bringing premium design, execution, and real results to every project in Harare and beyond.

Lennon Nyamajiwa is the founder and lead developer of Invitt Co — Harare's results-driven web design and digital marketing agency. He started Invitt Co after watching too many great Zimbabwe businesses stay invisible online, losing clients every day to competitors with a stronger digital presence.

What began as learning web development to build his father's business website — Topiary Marketing — quickly grew into a full-service agency serving businesses across Harare and Zimbabwe. From borehole drilling companies to marketing firms, Lennon has built websites that don't just look good but actually bring in customers.

Every project at Invitt Co is handled personally by Lennon — no outsourcing, no handoffs. He combines clean, conversion-focused design with solid technical SEO, making sure every website he builds is indexed on Google, found by the right customers, and built to last. His 14-day live guarantee is the only one of its kind in Harare.

Lennon's Philosophy
"A great website isn't just a digital business card — it's a 24/7 sales system. I don't build pages, I build engines that generate enquiries, establish credibility, and grow businesses while you focus on what you do best."

You've built a business you believe in. Let's make sure Harare knows about it.
