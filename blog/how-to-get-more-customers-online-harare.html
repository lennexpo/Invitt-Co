How to Get More Customers Online for Your Harare Business

By Invitt Co  ·  March 2026  ·  6 min read
← Back to Blog

Every business owner in Harare wants the same thing — more customers. The good news is that the internet has made it easier than ever to reach people who are actively looking for exactly what you offer. The challenge is knowing which strategies actually work for the Zimbabwe market.

Here are the most effective ways to attract more customers online for your Harare business, ranked by impact.

Strategies That Actually Work in Zimbabwe

01

Rank on Google for Local Searches

When someone in Harare searches for your type of business — "electrician in Harare", "bakery near me", "web designer Zimbabwe" — you want to be at the top. This happens through SEO: the right keywords on your website, a complete Google Business Profile, and consistent content. This is the highest-quality traffic you can get because these people are already looking to buy.

02

Optimise Your Google Business Profile

A fully completed Google Business Profile puts your business on Google Maps and in local search results for free. Add your address, phone number, business hours, photos, and a description packed with relevant keywords. Encourage happy customers to leave reviews — even one 5-star review can push you above competitors who have none.

03

Run Targeted Google Ads

Google Ads puts your business at the very top of search results instantly, before any organic results. You only pay when someone clicks. For businesses in Harare targeting specific services, even a small budget of $50–$100 USD per month can drive significant enquiries when set up correctly.

04

Use WhatsApp as a Sales Channel

WhatsApp is the dominant communication platform in Zimbabwe. Add a WhatsApp button to your website and make it easy for visitors to message you directly. Respond quickly and professionally. Many Harare businesses have built entire customer bases purely through WhatsApp enquiries driven from their website.

05

Post Consistently on Social Media

Facebook and Instagram are widely used in Zimbabwe. Regular posts showing your products, services, behind-the-scenes content, and customer testimonials build trust and keep your business top of mind. The goal is not to go viral — it's to be consistently visible to your target audience in Harare and beyond.

06

Ask for Referrals and Reviews

Word of mouth is still powerful in Zimbabwe — and online reviews are digital word of mouth. After completing a job or sale, ask your customer to leave a Google review. A business with 10 five-star reviews will always outperform one with none, even if the quality is identical. Make asking for reviews a standard part of your process.

The Common Thread

Every strategy above works better with a professional website as the foundation. Your website is where you send people from Google, social media, and WhatsApp. It's where they decide whether to contact you or go to a competitor. A poorly built or non-existent website undermines every other marketing effort you make.
